Initial release of Timeprint — personal screen time analytics for macOS and iOS.

• Rich analytics dashboard with daily, weekly, and monthly trends
• Apps & Categories breakdown showing where your time goes
• Calendar views with hour-by-hour breakdowns and heatmaps
• Session analysis and focus streak tracking
• Cross-device sync via iCloud
• Home Screen widgets for quick stats
• Export to CSV, JSON, and PDF
• Privacy-first: all data stays on your device
