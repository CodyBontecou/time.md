Timeprint gives you beautiful, detailed insights into your screen time — across all your Apple devices.

See exactly where your time goes with rich analytics, interactive charts, and heatmaps. No account required. No data leaves your device. Just honest, private screen time tracking.

HOW IT WORKS

On Mac, Timeprint reads Apple's built-in Screen Time database to surface your usage data in a way that's actually useful. On iPhone, it uses Apple's DeviceActivity framework to track usage natively. Daily summaries sync automatically via iCloud so you get the full picture in one place.

FEATURES

• Overview Dashboard — Today's total screen time, weekly trends, and top apps at a glance
• Trends — Daily, weekly, and monthly usage patterns with beautiful Swift Charts
• Apps & Categories — See which apps consume your time with detailed breakdowns
• Session Analysis — Understand your habits with session duration buckets
• Focus Streaks — Track your productivity momentum over time
• Calendar Views — Day, week, and month views with hour-by-hour breakdowns
• Heatmaps — Discover your most active hours and days visually
• Cross-Device Sync — View combined usage from Mac, iPhone, and iPad via iCloud
• Home Screen Widgets — Small, medium, and lock screen widgets for quick stats
• Export — Save your data as CSV, JSON, or PDF anytime

PRIVACY

Your privacy is not negotiable. Timeprint is built on these principles:

• Local-Only — All raw data stays on your device, always
• No Account Required — No sign-up, no tracking, no analytics
• No Server — There is no backend; iCloud is the only network service
• Sync is Optional — Use it on a single device without iCloud
• Open Source — Audit every line of code yourself
• Only daily summaries sync to iCloud — never individual sessions, timestamps, or browsing history

YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL

Timeprint doesn't sell your data because Timeprint doesn't have your data. Everything is processed locally on your device. Export or delete your data anytime.
