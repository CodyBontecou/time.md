Privacy Policy.

EFFECTIVE DATE: FEBRUARY 23, 2026

01 / OVERVIEW

Timeprint ("the App") is a macOS screen time analytics application built and operated by Isolated Tech ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles your information.

The short version: Timeprint is a local-only application. We do not collect, transmit, store, or process any of your personal data. Your screen time data never leaves your machine.

02 / DATA COLLECTION

Timeprint does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, account details)
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports
  • No device identifiers
  • No IP addresses
  • No cookies or tracking technologies

The App makes zero outbound network connections. There are no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, and no phone-home mechanisms of any kind embedded in the App.

03 / LOCAL DATA STORAGE

Timeprint reads screen time data from your Mac's local Screen Time database (provided by macOS) and stores processed analytics in a local SQLite database on your machine:

  • Screen time data: Read from the local screentime.db or knowledgeC.db maintained by macOS.
  • Category mappings: Stored at ~/Library/Application Support/Timeprint/category-mappings.db
  • Application preferences: Stored locally via macOS standard UserDefaults.

All data remains exclusively on your device. We have no access to it.

04 / BACKGROUND PROCESSING

Timeprint installs a local Launch Agent that syncs screen time data every 4 hours in the background, even when the App is not running. This process:

  • Runs entirely on your local machine
  • Reads only from local macOS databases
  • Writes only to local Timeprint databases
  • Makes no network connections

05 / THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Timeprint does not integrate with, connect to, or share data with any third-party services, APIs, or platforms.

06 / DATA EXPORT

The App allows you to export your screen time data in PNG, PDF, and CSV formats. These exports are generated locally and saved to a location you choose on your device. We do not have access to your exports.

07 / CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Timeprint does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no specific children's privacy concerns.

08 / CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes — we recommend checking this page periodically.
